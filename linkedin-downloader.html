Download LinkedIn profiles as PDFs with one click. Extract candidate data and export to CSV for seamless analysis.

Features

One-Click PDF Download

Download any LinkedIn profile as a PDF with a single click. A floating button appears on every profile page.

Data Extraction

Automatically extracts name, headline, experience, education, skills, and more from each profile.

CSV Export

Export all saved profiles to CSV format. Perfect for bulk candidate management and analysis.

Privacy First

All data stored locally in your browser. No data sent to external servers without your action.

Auto-Save

Profiles are automatically saved as you browse. Never lose candidate data again.

Tool Integration

Seamlessly integrates with Candidate Analyzer for job fit scoring and comparison.

Installation Guide

1

Download the Extension

Click the download button above to get the extension ZIP file. Extract it to a folder on your computer.

2

Open Chrome Extensions

Navigate to chrome://extensions/ in your Chrome browser. Enable "Developer mode" using the toggle in the top-right corner.

3

Load the Extension

Click "Load unpacked" and select the extracted folder. The LinkedIn Profile Downloader icon will appear in your toolbar.

4

Start Downloading

Visit any LinkedIn profile. Click the blue "Download Profile" button that appears in the bottom-right corner, or use the extension popup.
