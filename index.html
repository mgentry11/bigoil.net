Our Legacy

From the legendary oilfields of West Texas to operations spanning the globe, Big Oil has been at the forefront of energy production for over five decades.

Our commitment to innovation, safety, and responsible resource development has made us a trusted partner in powering the world's progress. We combine cutting-edge technology with deep geological expertise to maximize production while minimizing environmental impact.

Global Operations

Exploration

Advanced seismic imaging and AI-driven analysis identify promising reserves across multiple formations.

Production

State-of-the-art drilling and extraction technologies maximize yield while ensuring operational safety.

Refining

Modern refinery facilities transform crude oil into fuels and petrochemicals that power modern life.

Distribution

Global logistics network ensures reliable delivery to markets across six continents.

Committed to Tomorrow

We recognize our responsibility to balance energy production with environmental stewardship. Our comprehensive sustainability program includes:

  • Land restoration projects
